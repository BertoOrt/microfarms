Micro Farms Colorado

No Herbicides. No Pesticides. No Chemical Fertilizers
Micro Farms is turning people’s yards into hyper-local, organically grown food!

Who are we?

Micro Farms is an urban farming social-enterprise transforming underutilized homeowner resources into local and healthy food producing systems.

News Spotlight

Micro Farms has been featured on Denver Fox 31 News & Denver ABC 7 News

Become a Yard Partener

Micro Farms has a network of homeowners, our “Yard Partner.” Yard Partners have donated their land and pay for the water. We then install, maintain and harvest chemical-free, biodynamic gardens, where once there were lawns.

“Micro Farms has created an exciting sustainable business model that has taken local and organic food to a whole new level.”
-Anna Perks, Business Sustainability Advisor, Boulder County

Learn more...

Micro Farms’ Yard Partners do not have to worry about watering or weeding the garden (we take care of all that). You can rest easy because there is no lawn to mow throughout the year. In return for the land and water donation, we deliver a weekly basket of in-season produce grown and aggregated from all of our Micro Farms (estimated value: $850).

Micro Farms then sells the remaining healthy food at local, sustainably-minded restaurants, Food Co-ops, Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) Shares, Farmer’s Markets & Direct Sales. Sign up for our Newsletter to stay up to date with our Farmer’s Markets, CSA Program and get healthy food recipes and advice.
